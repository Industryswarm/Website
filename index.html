A platform for web and business applications, supply chain, commerce, CRM, Finances, Projects and more - including your own designs.

Services

IndustrySwarm Services is the core services platform of IndustrySwarm. It is a repository for all business objects and logic, membership management with RESTful API and WebSockets-based interfaces.

Pages

IndustrySwarm Pages allows you to create content and logic-rich applications for web and native devices.

CRM

IndustrySwarm CRM is an application for Customer Relationship Management for Sales for B2B Customers, Investor relationships, Strategic Partner / Channel Relationships, Competitor Relationships and more.

ProductPipes

IndustrySwarm ProductPipes provides a comprehensive platform for supply chain, product management and both online and in-store commerce.

Finances

IndustrySwarm Finances allows you to manage your personal or organisation's books in the cloud.

Projects

IndustrySwarm Projects allows you to create and manage projects in the cloud, create and execute tickets, assign resources and work in whichever project methodology works best for you - whether that be agile methods like Kanban and Scrum, or something more traditional.

GraphCX

IndustrySwarm GraphCX provides an interface for managing both core and custom IndustrySwarm data objects within a graph spreadsheet. That is - all business objects are represented as nodes on a graph with the relationships between them represented as edges. Attibutes/values for those business objects can be static data from the database or calculated values based on Formula attributes - with the ability to have calculated values for each node dependent on a combination of static values and calculated values on the connected nodes in a flow-on fashion.

Helpdesk

IndustrySwarm Helpdesk allows you to support cases for your B2C customers through a multi-channel system of email, web forms, phone, social and more.

LivingData.me

IndustrySwarm LivingData.me provides a comprehensive data warehousing, analytics and trend analysis system with machine/deep learning for both companies and individuals.

MyJSLab

MyJSLab is a set of tools for Life Sciences (cheminformatics, bioinformatics, genetics) and Engineering.

People

IndustrySwarm People is an application for managing human resources within your business, including organisational structure, performance planning, training, interviews, onboarding, exits and more.

Processes

IndustrySwarm Processes is an application for managing your business processes and workflow - both those that are primarily manual and those with automated components.
